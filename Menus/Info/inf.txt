Furnicor is a company that designs and sells ready-to-assemble furniture, 
kitchen appliances and home accessories, among other goods and home services. 
It currently has over 650,000 members worldwide. As Furnicor Family member, clients can enjoy many benefits, 
such as discounts on lots of products and free access to workshops and events, 
return their products indefinitely, get a free 14-day 'oops'-insurance, 
take a free cup of coffee or tea at the restaurant, etc. In order to enjoy all these exciting member benefits, 
a client must be a family member of Furnicor. They can be registered for free at a customer service desk.
This membership management system facilitates the registration of members and the retrieving and adjusting the data of 
members and employees of Furnicor.

To use this functionality, please login using your credentials or contact the administrator to create an account.
